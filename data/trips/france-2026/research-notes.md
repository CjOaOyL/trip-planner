  - Avoids CDG connection, saves a full layover day
- Alternative researched: Brussels Airlines (via BRU) — flagged as poor legroom on short-haul A319 leg (30" pitch)

### Flight Selection Priorities
- **Legroom is a top priority** — family of 4, transatlantic overnight
- Minimum 34" pitch (Economy Plus or better on United; Premium Economy on other carriers)
- Basic Economy = no-go (no seat selection, assigned at check-in)
- Kids do NOT need to be in same cabin class as parents, but same row/adjacent seat preferred
- On United 767-300: Economy Plus (34-37") is in the same cabin as Economy but rows 20-35 area; Premium Plus (2-3-2, 38") is a separate forward cabin

### Chase Sapphire Transfers
- 1:1 to United MileagePlus ✅

## Activities by City

### Chamonix (Jul 28–30)
- Aiguille du Midi cable car + ice cave (iconic, book ahead)
- Mer de Glace glacier — cog railway (Montenvers train) + glacier walk
- Tandem paragliding (launches from Planpraz, very popular in summer)
- Chamonix Adventure Park — zip lines, tree climbing (good for kids)
- Mountain biking (lifts run in summer with bikes)
- White water rafting on the Arve River
- Albert 1er dinner (Michelin, see above)
- Concert #1 is in Martigny, Switzerland (Jul 29) — ~45 min drive from Chamonix

### Arles (Jul 30–Aug 3)
- **Les Rencontres d'Arles** (photography festival) — runs all summer, exhibitions citywide; one of the world's biggest photography events. Dates overlap with stay perfectly.
- Les Arènes (Roman amphitheater) — summer bullfighting and concerts
- Fondation Vincent van Gogh Arles
- Camargue day trip (~45 min drive) — flamingos, white horses, black bulls, beach at Les Saintes-Maries-de-la-Mer
- Saturday market — Boulevard des Lices (massive)
- Théâtre Antique, Les Alyscamps, Cloître Saint-Trophime

### Nice (Aug 3–6)
- Beaches + water sports along Promenade des Anglais
- Vieux-Nice (Old Town) + Cours Saleya flower/food market
- Castle Hill (Colline du Château) — free, panoramic views, playground, waterfall
- Day trips: Monaco (~30 min), Èze village, Antibes, Villefranche-sur-Mer, Cap Ferrat (Villa Ephrussi de Rothschild)
- Musée Matisse, Musée Marc Chagall, MAMAC
